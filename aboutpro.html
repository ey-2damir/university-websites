Information Technology


    ABOUT PROGRAM

    ICT specialty is one of the most important local market requirements in modern era as the 
    new growing challenges all over the world need advanced skills to cover modern industry 
    requirements based on using computers and digital techniques.
    Computer systems, applications and techniques are involved in all aspects of life.
    So the need of trained professionals on computer applications is essential to keep up 
    the informational revolution and fill work market demands.
    Information and Communication Technology Dept. 
    Consists of different sets of curriculums to cover different computer applications ,
    it contains of programming and researching applications sets as well that aim to solve local market real problems.
    This department enables students to acquire the needed knowledge and skills in order to be able to analyze,
    develop and program ICT systems in any organization and governmental or private authorities.
